Students compared weekly workload estimates across several courses. For example, Intro Programming sections reported 6-8 hours per week for students who had some prior experience, while Data Structures and Algorithms were commonly reported at 10-15 hours per week. Project-heavy courses (software engineering-style) sometimes peaked at 20+ hours during project weeks.

Practical advice: if you are balancing multiple CS courses, try to avoid two simultaneous project-heavy classes where possible, and check recent term forums for updated workload reports before finalizing your schedule.

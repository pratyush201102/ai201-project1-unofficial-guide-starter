Discussion threads focused on grading turnaround times and feedback usefulness. Some students praised Professor Kim's course for quick (3-5 day) assignment grading and detailed comments, while others complained of 2+ week delays with minimal feedback. A typical suggestion was to ask for sample feedback during office hours if your graded submission lacks comments.

Several posts recommended keeping a private log of assignment rubrics and common mistakes to avoid repeating the same errors in future submissions.

# Unofficial Department FAQ

## Source notes
- Summarize student-maintained FAQ items that affect course planning.
- Include policy-specific statements (attendance, office hours, grading turnaround).

## Collected text
### Attendance and Participation

Many courses state attendance as optional, but several students reported that "participation hints" on exams come directly from in-class examples. The unofficial FAQ recommends attending labs and review sessions those weeks.

### Grading Turnaround

Typical grading turnaround for written assignments is between one and two weeks. If an assignment exceeds this, students recommended contacting the course coordinator early.

### Extensions and Late Policy

The FAQ notes that TA-granted extensions are common for documented emergencies, but that requests should be made at least 24 hours before deadlines when possible.
